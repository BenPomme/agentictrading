# cross_venue_probability_elasticity

## Active Goal
We believe we can create alpha by modeling the nonlinear elasticity between centralized exchange spot returns and delayed repricing in binary prediction markets, then trading only when the prediction market's implied probability surface underreacts relative to a locally estimated mapping from underlying price shocks to contract fair value.
